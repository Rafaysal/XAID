The network decides the applicant’s
score on a scale of 0-100%.
A score of 50% or greater is accepted,
lower is rejected.
The network decides the score based on 7
features of the applicants. The average
score of these 159 applicants is 75%
The network splits applicants into
4 groups based on their similarities.
Within each group, it believes these
applicants are similar in terms of
whether they should be accepted or rejected.
Hover over the groups to see their
average score and a description of
the group.
One way we can see if this Neural Network’s
decisions are impacted by gender is by
flipping every applicant’s gender and see how their
score changes.
